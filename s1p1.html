Locked In

You wake up one morning without the energy or motivation to do anything but you get an email notifcation that says your Amazon package is finally arriving. You are very excited as you have been waiting for this package for 6 weeks. Unfortunately, you have a neighbor that is stealing all of your packages and to ensure this doesn't happen this time, your housemate has set up a series of booby traps so that the neighbor gets caught. Just one thing, she hasn't told you how to avoid them... figure out the way to avoid all the booby traps but to leave them still intact so that your neighbor doesn't steal the package. Ready! Set! Go!
